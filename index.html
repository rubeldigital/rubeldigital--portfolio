Common Reasons for GMC Suspension

  • Misrepresentation (Main reason for suspension)
  • Insufficient Contact Information
  • Missing Return & Refund Policies
  • Inaccurate Tax & Shipping settings
  • Website Needs Improvements (UI/UX issues)
  • Circumventing Systems & Policy Violations
  • Counterfeit Goods or Prohibited Content
  • Unacceptable Business Practices

Our Professional Process to Fix GMC

  • Deep Audit: Checking every single policy of Google.
  • Website Cleanup: Fixing footer, pages, and checkout bugs.
  • Feed Repair: Updating titles, descriptions, and GTINs.
  • Policy Alignment: Re-writing refund & shipping policies.
  • Official Appeal: Submitting professional reinstatement request.
  • Monitoring: Continuous tracking until account is approved.
